About The Band

Crimson Voltage brings raw rock energy with a modern edge. From underground clubs to sold-out arenas, our sound is an electrifying mix of soaring guitar solos, thunderous drums, and gritty vocals. Since 2014, we’ve been igniting stages around the globe.

Alex “The Riff” Johnson – Lead Guitar

Master of electrifying solos and face-melting riffs. Alex shreds through the silence and lights up every stage.

Sam “Thunder” Lee – Drums

From whisper-quiet rhythms to thunderous rolls, Sam’s heartbeat drives every Crimson Voltage performance.

Maya “Echo” Rivers – Vocals

A voice that cuts through the noise — Maya’s gritty highs and soulful lows define the Crimson Voltage sound.
